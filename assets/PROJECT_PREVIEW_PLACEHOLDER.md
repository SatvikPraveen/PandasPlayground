## 📸 Screenshot Tips

### Good Screenshot Includes:
- ✅ Clear, readable charts
- ✅ Visible KPIs and metrics
- ✅ Professional color scheme
- ✅ No personal information
- ✅ High resolution (at least 1200px wide)

### Avoid:
- ❌ Blurry or pixelated images
- ❌ Dark mode (unless consistent)
- ❌ Browser UI (take app-only screenshot)
- ❌ Error messages or loading states
